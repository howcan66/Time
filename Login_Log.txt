LOGIN_LOG - Password Protection Audit Trail (HJ-ID1RS5)
================================================================================

PURPOSE: Track all login attempts, successes, failures, and lockouts for security

FORMAT: [Timestamp] | Username | Status | Attempt # | Notes

STATUS VALUES:
  SUCCESS  - Valid credentials accepted
  FAILED   - Invalid credentials rejected
  LOCKED   - Account locked after 3 failed attempts (5-minute timeout)
  LOGOUT   - User manually logged out
