‚úÖ SCENARIO 2: Accidental Lockout Recovery

   3. Uses password toggle to verify correct entry

   5. Logs in successfully without lockout

‚úÖ SCENARIO 3: Lockout After 5 Attempts

   3. Login button disabled
   4. Click "Reset Lockout" button
